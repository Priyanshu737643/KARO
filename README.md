# Karo Pitch – Demo Landing Page

## Project Overview

This project is a demo landing page created as part of the **Karo Pitch Intern Assignment** by **KaroStartup**.

Karo Pitch is an initiative designed to help **early-stage founders across India**, especially from **Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities**, pitch their startups directly to investors.
The goal of this landing page is to clearly communicate the vision of the platform and encourage founders to apply for pitching opportunities.

This project demonstrates **product thinking, UI design, and the ability to translate a startup concept into a simple, user-friendly web interface.**

---

## Website Sections

The homepage includes the following sections:

### 1. Hero Section

A strong headline introducing the platform:

**“Pitch Your Startup to India’s Top Investors.”**

Includes two call-to-action buttons:

* Apply to Pitch
* Explore Startups

---

### 2. About Karo Pitch

Explains the purpose of the platform and how it helps founders gain access to investors, mentorship, and visibility.

---

### 3. How It Works

A simple four-step process showing how founders can participate:

1. Apply with your pitch deck
2. Get shortlisted by KaroStartup
3. Pitch live to investors
4. Raise funding and scale

---

### 4. Who Can Apply

Lists the types of startups that can participate, such as:

* D2C Brands
* Consumer Startups
* MSMEs
* SaaS Startups
* Manufacturing Businesses
* Bharat-focused Startups

---

### 5. Investors Section

Highlights that investors and mentors will participate in the platform and discover promising startups.

---

### 6. Featured Startups

Displays example startup cards with dummy content to demonstrate how startups may appear on the platform.

---

### 7. About KaroStartup

Introduces KaroStartup as a startup storytelling platform that has published thousands of founder stories and built a large community of entrepreneurs.

---

### 8. Final Call To Action

Encourages founders to apply with the message:

**“Ready to Pitch Your Startup?”**

Includes buttons:

* Apply Now
* Partner With Us

---

## Technologies Used

This demo website was built using simple web technologies:

* **HTML5** – structure of the webpage
* **CSS3** – styling and layout
* **JavaScript** – basic interactivity

The project was intentionally kept simple to focus on **design clarity and user experience.**

## Design Approach

The landing page was designed with the following goals:

* **Clean and modern user interface**
* **Startup ecosystem theme**
* **Clear explanation of the platform’s value**
* **Easy navigation and readability**
* **Mobile-friendly layout**

The design uses card layouts, simple sections, and clear typography to ensure that visitors quickly understand how the platform works and how they can apply.
